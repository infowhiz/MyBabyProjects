About Mom

My mom was a highly versatile person. You would love her. She was an awesome mom, an extraordinary cook, a master gardener. And you know what? Being a family person didn't keep her having a successful career. She was holding a masters in social anthropology. She had been started her career as an economic development planning specialist for government. Once she had been head of the office of arts & culture of a megalopolis. She also had served as general secretary of multiple highly esteemed associations. These are only some of her great qualities that come to my mind at a glance. But other than that, you could talk with her about almost anything. She was an intellectual, smart, nice and fun person to be around. Hard to believe right? I miss her.. Can you tell?
